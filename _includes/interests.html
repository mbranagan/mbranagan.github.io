Interests

My personal interests include:

  • Animal Welfare. I am an avid animal lover and vegan. In my spare time I enjoy spoiling my two cats, volunteering for local animal shelters, and trips to the zoo. The majority of my volunteering experience has been through the San Diego Humane Society.
  • Music. Growing up I learned to play the piano and guitar. Today I love spending time discovering new artists, collecting vinyls, and attending local shows.
  • Sports. Living an active lifestyle is important to me. I enjoy trying new things: yoga, group fitness classes, and practiced Muay Thai for a year, but my long term favorites are hiking and running. Born and raised in Massachusetts, I am additionally a Boston sports fan.
